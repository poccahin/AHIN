# Phase 5A Legal and Compliance Checklist

## Purpose

This checklist identifies legal and compliance questions that must be answered before any future Phase 5B micro-transfer. It is not legal advice and does not authorize live execution.

## Required Review Areas

```text
jurisdiction
entity authority
asset classification
user disclosure
risk disclosure
sanctions screening
blocked jurisdiction screening
tax/accounting treatment
data retention
privacy
consumer protection
market abuse risk
token transfer restrictions
```

## LIFE++ Specific Questions

```text
Is LIFE++ transferability unrestricted for the planned action?
Does the action imply sale, redemption, yield, or investment return?
Does the action create new issuance? Expected answer: no.
Does the action require additional holder disclosure?
Does the fixed supply statement remain accurate?
```

## Operator Assertions

Before Phase 5B, operator must confirm:

```text
no sanctioned party involvement
no blocked jurisdiction involvement
no private key custody ambiguity
no misleading production claim
no root domain takeover bundled with asset movement
no burn action without separate legal approval
```

## Records

Retain:

```text
approval notes
wallet allowlist records
cap policy approval
decoded transaction summary
simulation output
rollback plan
post-transfer replay attestation
```

## Phase 5A Boundary

Phase 5A may document compliance readiness only. Any compliance approval for live movement must be revalidated in Phase 5B with final transaction details.
